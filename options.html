Area Links
Settings

How to use: Hold down Alt + Z (by default) and drag a box with your left mouse button over the links you want to open.

To copy links: Hold down Alt + X (by default) and drag a box to copy all links in the area to the clipboard.

To customize shortcuts: Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts.
